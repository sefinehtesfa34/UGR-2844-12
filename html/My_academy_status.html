My history of the eduction systems up to now

This is my pleasure to share you my experiences and my backgrounds related to my education systems from level one up to now (December 2021). First I am super excited to tell you my backgrounds. Now let me show you from the beginning of my education systems up to now.

I was 7 years old when I came to school for the first time. My father is a farmer who is very very hard working man. However, It is not ideal to say that he is like a proficient at that level befor 15 years. He is very wise. My dad was one of the bearocrates in the regime of Derg.

A bureaucrat is a member of a bureaucracy and can compose the administration of any organization of any size. He was teaching and administering a large goups of people. He brought me in a fantastic way as he advised me a lot. I don't have sound to say what he was doing for me. At the first time my dad invited me to Hamus Wenz primary school.

Hamus Wenz primary school is located in amhara region at sekela wereda. More specifically it is located at Taliya kebele. The primary school was up to grade 8 levels. In my ministery regional exam I had got 92 percentiles which was 72 out of 100.

From grade 9 to grade 12, I was the first in my sections all of the semesters. In the first semester, one student leveled above me but I leveled above him in the next semester and then in CGPA, I was the first. In matrices, I have got 4 that was fantastic for my families. They felt that I would be among the top rank students.

However, with is a short period of time their thought was real and achieved. I was the first in my secondary school grade 12 Entrance National Exam. I have got 611 out of 700. I have got a reward from the regional government. I recieved the reward in Bahir Dar, the capital of Amhara region.

The specific place I recieved the reward was at GROUND HOTEL, near Lake Tana. The reward was 5000 birr from the regional government and personal computer from ASRAT Non-governmental Organization. Unfortunately, I didn't recieve the personal computer as that was limited relative to the number of students. But,I recieved my money reward.

In 2019, I came to Addis Ababa University as a fresh man level. In the first semester, I have studied at SEFERE SELAM COMPUS, the branches of TIKIUR ANBESSA. I have got 3.79 in the first semester of fresh man courses. In the next semester, I have selected Engineering department.

Then I came to Addis Ababa Institute of Technology, at 5 kilo compus. I have got 3.96 in that semesters. As a second year Engineering student, in the first semester, I have got 3.85 out of 4. After that I selected my interest field among Engineering fields. My interest was to become a software engineer. I have got my interest's field.

Now I am a 3rd year Software Engineering student at Addis Ababa Institute of Technology. Now, preferred interest is to become an Artificial Intelligence Engineer. I expect I will achieve my future career goals as I have many more backgrounds to become an Artificial Intelligence Engineer. Thanks for your passions to see my educational systems history.
